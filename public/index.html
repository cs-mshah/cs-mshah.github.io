I am a computer science graduate from IIT Mandi. I have worked as a research assistant at the Vision and AI Lab, IISc under the mentorship of Prof. Venkatesh Babu, where I worked on diffusion models and 3D gaussian splatting. Prior to that, I worked at Cashfree Payments as a software development engineer.

I am interested in working on challenging problems in computer vision and deep learning. I also maintain this blog to jot down my learnings, experiences and thoughts. I feel that writing helps me to solidify different concepts and share some insights with others. Feel free to contact me if you want to discuss interesting ideas.
